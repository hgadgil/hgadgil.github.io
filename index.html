About

As of Apr 2012, I've moved to the services team of open source PaaS - Cloudfoundry

In Jan 2009, I moved to the storage team on the Microsoft Windows Azure Platform where I worked on several core features related to Azure Blobs and Geo Replication, Upgrade infrastructure for Windows Azure Storage and Storage Analytics.

Prior to working at Microsoft, I finished my Ph.D. from Indiana University,  Bloomington in 2007. At Bloomington, I worked in the Community Grids Lab on the NaradaBrokering messaging middleware.

In 2006, while working on my thesis, I moved to the Distributed Systems Engineering group at Amazon.com, where I contributed to developing the next generation messaging middleware for supporting Amazon's internal service-oriented architecture as well as for Simple Queue Service (SQS), an Amazon Web Services offering.

My primary research interest is in the area of distributed systems.
